### Get Your Analysis

1. Click **"Analyze Chart"**
2. Wait 10-30 seconds for AI processing
3. **Results include**:
   - Direction: LONG, SHORT, or NEUTRAL
   - Entry price
   - Stop loss level
   - Take profit levels (TP1, TP2, TP3)
   - Risk/Reward ratio
   - Confidence score
   - Detailed reasoning
   - Market context

### Understanding Your Results

**Direction**:
- **LONG** = Buy signal (price expected to go up)
- **SHORT** = Sell signal (price expected to go down)  
- **NEUTRAL** = No clear trend, wait for better setup

**Entry**: Recommended price to enter the trade

**Stop Loss**: Price level to exit if trade goes against you (limits loss)

**Take Profit**: Price levels to take profits (TP1 = first target, TP2 = second, TP3 = final)

**Risk/Reward Ratio**: How much you can gain vs. how much you risk  
- Example: **R:R 1:3** means risk $1 to potentially gain $3
- Higher is better! Aim for at least 1:2

**Confidence Score**: AI's confidence in the analysis (0-100%)
- 80-100% = High confidence
- 60-79% = Medium confidence
- Below 60% = Low confidence (be cautious)

### Golden Rules

1. **Never risk more than 1-3% per trade**
   - Protects you from big losses
   - Allows you to survive losing streaks

2. **Always use stop losses**
   - No exceptions
   - Set it and leave it (don't move it)

3. **Don't overtrade**
   - Quality > quantity
   - 1-3 good trades per week is enough

4. **Start small**
   - Test strategies with small size
   - Increase as you gain confidence

5. **Keep a journal**
   - Track every trade (entry, exit, reason, emotion)
   - Review monthly to improve
