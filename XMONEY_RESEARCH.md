## Executive Summary

**xMoney** is the payments division of **MultiversX** (formerly Elrond Network). They're building a **compliant, regulated payment infrastructure** that bridges crypto and fiat for global payments.

**BIG NEWS:** Launching **3 MiCA-regulated stablecoins** in **June 2026**!
